DGS has people all over the world working together to test games and consoles. Right now all the people that work for DGS are working for free until we can really get the Studio up and running so that the workers can start earning money for their work.







We have people in the US, Canada, and the UK. All the workers are aging from 16 to 24 years old. We all met on Xbox live around 2 years ago. We are basically one big family. Most of us call each other by our gamertags but we only call a few people by their real name or the name they asked us to call them.







At Diesel Gaming Studio we accept people with disabilities because all of the people working for DGS have some sort of disability and because of that we understand each other and other people with those disablitites. We also accept people who have been in the Miltary. Some of the people who are working for DGS have been or are in the Miltary at this time.
